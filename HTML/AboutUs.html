About Us

So anyone who knows me personally, will know that I’ve always been obsessed with the city of London, The Royal Family (my mom absolutely adored Diana) and almost all things British. My favorite shopping memories date back to me as a 10 year old kid exploring the aisles of Hamleys on Regent Street and Harrods in Knightsbridge. When selecting our range of ladies clothing, we’re definitely inspired by the streets of Carnaby and Oxford, and the lines of Selfridges and Debenhams. Bringing you fashion-forward pieces, inspired by the heart of London shopping, Oxford Street and the birthplace of the fashion and cultural revolution, Carnaby. Oxford Street Apparel stocks casual European inspired pieces, dressy occasional wear and caters for the modern Hijaabi as well.


- Ayesha Lambat Ganchi (Owner & Founder)
